SUBJECTS

QAIDA
Noorani Qaida is a book for beginners to learn quanic arabic.Qaida means foundation.it teaches the correct pronunciation of the arabic alphabets.
HIFE-QURAN
Hife-quran course help the students to memorize the whole or a part of the holy quran.
DEENIYATH
Deeniyath is a theological system.It is a particular system or school of religious beliefs and teachings.
TAFSEERE-QURAN
Tafseer Quran is to help people understand the message and commands of Allah(SWT) very well to abide by them.It endeavors to facilities learn Quran memorization.
ARABIC
Everyone must learn to read and speak Arabic, the language of the prophet,in order to be able to understand and interpret the word of Allah through prayer and reading theQuran.
HADEES
Hadees is the record of actions,words and way of living of the Prophet Mohammad(PBUH).It is the second greatest and most important source in islam.
